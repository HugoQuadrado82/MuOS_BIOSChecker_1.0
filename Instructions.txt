Copy:
bioschecker.sh to /mnt/mmc/MUOS/application
.bioschecker (folder) to /mnt/mmc/MUOS/application
bios.png to /mnt/mmc/MUOS/theme/active/glyph/muxapp/

Run bioschecker from application menu
Press scan to scan the BIOS from your system.
The BIOS folder is located: /mnt/mmc/MUOS/bios

The BIOS package is based on: BATOCERA-V40
